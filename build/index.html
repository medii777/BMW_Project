BMW, short for Bayerische Motoren Werke means Bavarian Motor Works, is a German automobile manufacturer renowned for its blend of performance, luxury, and engineering excellence. From its origins in aircraft engine production during World War I, BMW transitioned to motorcycles and then automobiles, establishing itself as a producer of high-quality, sporty vehicles. Throughout its history, BMW has been characterized by a dedication to driving dynamics, often emphasizing rear-wheel drive and finely tuned suspensions to deliver a thrilling and engaging driving experience.
